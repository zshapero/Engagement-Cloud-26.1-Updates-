What You'll Learn

This course covers the following topics across six lessons.

  • What is Engagement Cloud Plus — Overview of how EC Plus enriches subscriber insights with demographic, psychographic, and geographic data to accelerate subscriber acquisition.
  • Working with Geographies — Demographic and psychographic data types, audience segments, micro-geographic targeting with ZIP+4, and defining your base area.
  • Finding and Acquiring New Subscribers — AI-driven lookalike segments, Facebook lookalike audiences, ACP targeting, and uploading prospect lists.
  • Planning Service Area Expansions — Using lookalike segments to prioritize new builds and network growth in highly favorable areas.
  • Grow Adoption of Managed Service Offerings — Targeting subscribers and prospects with relevant managed service offers using EC Plus filters.
  • Resources and Knowledge Check — Demo, additional resources, and a 5-question knowledge check to certify your understanding.
